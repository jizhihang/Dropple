CC	coordinating conjunction
CD	cardinal digit
DT	Determiner. Words which come beginning of a noun phrase. Ex. the, my, your, his, this, that, these, which
EX	existential there (like: "there is" ... think of it like "there exists")
FW	foreign word
IN	preposition/subordinating conjunction. Preposition: location or location in time. Ex. above, since, near. Subordinating conjunction: Non-complete
                                           thought. Ex. If, as, for
JJ	adjective. Describe a noun or a pronoun. Can specify the quality, the size, and the number of nouns or pronouns. Ex. big, two, huge
JJR	adjective. Comparative. Ex. bigger
JJS	adjective. Superlative. Ex. biggest
LS	list marker	Ex. 1)
MD	modal. Verb that is used to express ability, possibility, permission or obligation. Ex. could, will
NN	noun, singular. Person, place, things, animals, places, ideas, events. Ex. Tom Hanks, dogs, birthday
NNS	noun plural	'desks'
NNP	proper noun. Always start with a capital letter and refers to specific names of persons, places, or things. Ex. Game of Thrones
NNPS	proper noun, plural	'Americans'
PDT	predeterminer	'all the kids'
POS	possessive ending Ex. parent's
PRP	personal pronoun. Ex. I, he, she, we
PRP$	possessive pronoun	my, his, hers
RB	adverb.	Describe adjectives, verbs. Ex. danced gracefully.
RBR	adverb. Comparative. Ex. ran better
RBS	adverb. Superlative. Ex. ran best
RP	particle	give up
TO	to	go 'to' the store.
UH	interjection	errrrrrrrm
VB	verb. Shows action (physical or mental). Ex. missed, think, run
VBD	verb. Past tense. Ex. took
VBG	verb. -ing. Ex. taking
VBN	verb. Past participle. -en. Ex. taken
VBP	verb. Present, non-3d. Ex. take
VBZ	verb. 3rd person present. Ex. takes
WDT	wh-determiner	which
WP	wh-pronoun	who, what
WP$	possessive wh-pronoun	whose
WRB	wh-abverb	where, when
